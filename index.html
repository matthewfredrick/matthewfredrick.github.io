About Me

I'm a student-athlete at Brigham Young University, competing in Division I football while pursuing a pre-business degree focused on finance. The same drive that pushes me through two-a-days pushes me to understand markets, build wealth, and make a meaningful impact with numbers.

Before returning to BYU, I spent two years as a missionary in the Republic of Kiribati — a remote island nation in the Pacific. That experience reshaped how I see the world, taught me to adapt under pressure, and gave me a deep appreciation for community. Now I'm channeling that energy into a new internship with Blue Unicorn (a protein bar brand), growing my NIL presence, and building the life I've always dreamed of.
